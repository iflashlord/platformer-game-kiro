# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good faith; seek to understand before criticizing.
- Use inclusive language; be kind to newcomers.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or sustained disruption.
- Doxxing or sharing othersâ€™ private information.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private issue or contact the maintainer. Provide context (links, screenshots) where possible. Reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings or temporary/permanent bans from community spaces.
